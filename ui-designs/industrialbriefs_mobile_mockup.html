AI Concrete Monitoring Reduces Construction Waste by 30%
ConcreteAI's sensor platform flags curing anomalies before failure — cutting rework costs on bridge decks and tunnels.
IndustrialBriefs Newsroom · Mar 14, 2026 · 4 min read

ConcreteAI sensor array, Chicago bridge deck. Photo: ConcreteAI

A new AI monitoring system from ConcreteAI is reducing material waste by up to 30% by catching curing anomalies before they escalate into full rework events.

The platform uses embedded IoT sensors combined with a machine learning model trained on 4 million concrete pour data points.

Advertisement
300×250 · Deferred load

How the system works

Sensors transmit readings every 30 seconds. The AI model compares incoming data against cure curve baselines for the specific mix design and ambient conditions.

"We caught a cold joint forming at 3am — something that would have required a full deck section removal at inspection." — Walsh Construction PM
